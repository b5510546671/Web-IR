recommendation procedure

  1. please check the ku library /eco library catalog .
  2. log in to the system using only nontri account and password; library reserves the right to use this service for only faculty, students and staff of kasetsart university.

  3. complete the form with the details of the resource recommended and the details for all fields having the symbol *, which means they must be filled in and the information is important for verifying; library reserves the right to cancel any recommendation of which the details are incomplete or unclear.
  4. the ku library will consider selecting new resources based on the library resource development policy by limiting the recommendations as follows:

    books 10 titles per person per year (1 october - 30 september)
    journals unlimited, based on purchasing policy and budget.
    audiovisuals
