general information

 

            a small and attractive nursing college, bcnnv is a trendsetter in progressive teaching methods. serving as the lighthouse nursing college for the bcn system, the faculty helps its students to develop and improve humanistic life-long learning skills. bcnnv collaborates with, and is affiliated to, kasetsart university and operates graduate nursing programmes.
