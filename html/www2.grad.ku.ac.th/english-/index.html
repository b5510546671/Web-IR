my background


name: oliver unlamai
country:     united kingdom

i was born and spent the first 8 years of my life in austria. it’s a beautiful and peaceful small country in central europe, famous for classical music (mozart), mountains and arnold schwarzenegger! after that i lived, studied and worked in england for around 20 years. after so long in england i feel very british – i love fish and chips and like to complain about the weather. in fact i am actually half thai and came on many holidays to thailand throughout my life. i always wanted to live here, so in 2009 i moved here permanently. i think it was a great decision as i am very happy here.


i have a bachelor’s degree in international business management from aston university, which is in the city of birmingham in england. after graduating i worked in investment banking for a few years, including one year in one of the most famous financial hubs of the world, canary wharf in london. it was certainly an interesting experience. however i prefer teaching and i love thailand, so i combined the two and have been an english teacher in thailand for the last 6 years. i love meeting so many different students, interacting with them and helping them to improve such a vital skill as the english language.

my biggest interest is probably football. i love to play it, to watch it and to read about it. now that i’m getting a bit older i would like to coach and be a manager. i also enjoy travelling: i have been to many european countries and now that i live in thailand i plan to travel around asia. in the future i would also love to visit north and south america.
