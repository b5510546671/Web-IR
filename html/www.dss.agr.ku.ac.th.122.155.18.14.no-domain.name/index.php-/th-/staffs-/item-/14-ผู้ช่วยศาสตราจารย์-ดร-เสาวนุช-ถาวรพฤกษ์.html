ความเชี่ยวชาญ การสำรวจและจำแนกดิน, การวางแผนการใช้ที่ดิน, แร่วิทยาและธรณีเคมีของดินและตะกอน

 

บทความเด่น

khunthong m. and s. tawornpruek. 2012. estimation of soil organic matter from some soil properties and soil organic meter test kit in thailand. 38th congress on science and technology of thailand.

gilkes, r., s. tawornpruek, n. prakongkep, c. scanlan, w. tichbon and a. aboagye. 2012. caustic insoluble aluminium containing nanominerals in bauxite from south western australia. proceedings of the 9th international alumina quality workshop. perth convention and exhibition centre from sunday 18 - thursday 22 march 2012.

janjirawuttikul, n., m. umitsu and s. tawornpruek.  2011.  pedogenesis of acid sulfate soil in the lower central plain of thailand.  international journal of soil science 6(2): 77-102.

tawornpruek, s., i. kheoruenromne, a. suddhiprakarn and r.j. gilkes. 2005. microstructure and water retention of oxisols in thailand. australian journal of soil research. 43: 973-986.

tawornpruek, s., a. suddhiprakarn and i. kheoruenromne. 2005. the potential of quartzipsamments for sugarcane growing in southeast coast, thailand. international conference on management of tropical sandy soils for sustainable agriculture, 27th november – 2nd december, 2005, khon kaen, thailand.

tawornpruek, s., i. kheoruenromne, a. suddhiprakarn and r.j. gilkes. 2006. an approach on soils management practices for red oxisols under tropical savanna climate in thailand, pp. 153-160. the proceedings of 44th kasetsart university annual conference (subject: plant), 30th january – 2nd february, 2006.

tawornpruek, s., i. kheoruenromne, a. suddhiprakarn and r.j. gilkes. 2006. properties of red oxisols on calcareous sedimentary rocks in thailand. geoderma. 136: 477-493.

tawornpruek, s. and r.j. gilkes. 2008. the composition and structural properties of iron oxides in lateritic bauxite in the darling range, western australia. 2008. annual meeting of the clay minerals society in collaboration with the division of geochemistry, 235th acs national meeting, new orleans, la, april 6-10, 2008.

ยุพเยาว์ หัสจรรย์,เสาวนุช ถาวรพฤกษ์ และ เอิบ เขียวรื่นรมณ์. 2551. ลักษณะการดูดยึดน้ำ และสมการถ่ายทอดสมบัติของดินภายใต้ความชื้นที่แตกต่างกันบนพื้นที่สูง. วารสารดินและปุ๋ย 30: 280-292.

2011 site-specific nutrient management for capsicum frutescens in major production areas of nakhon ratchasima province.

2013 development soil quality and productivity index for sugarcane production in eastern thailand.
