current researches:

2012 : assessment of 13c isotope in soil and plant system relating nitrogen and water uptake by plants in hilly

              cropping system

บทความเด่น

chittamart, n., a. suddhiprakarn, i. kheoruenromne and r.j. gilkes.  2010.  the pedo-geochemistry of vertisols under tropical savanna climate.  geoderma 159 (3-4): 304-316.
chittamart, n., a. suddhiprakarn, i. kheoruenromne and r.j. gilkes.  2010.  beidellite in tropical savanna soils.  in g.j. churchman, j.l. keeling and p.g. self, eds.  extended abstract of the australian clay minerals society conference, 7-8th august, qut gardens point, brisbane, queensland.
chittamart, n., a. suddhiprakarn, i. kheoruenromne and r.j. gilkes. 2010.  charge properties and potassium fixation by clays from thai vertisols, pp. 26-29.  in r.j. gilkes and n. prakongkep, eds.  proceedings of the 19th world congress of soil science; soil solutions for a changing world; isbn 978-0-646-53783-2: published on dvd; http://www.iuss.org; symposium 2.4.1; soil minerals and soil sustainability; 2010 aug 1-6. brisbane, australia: iuss.
chittamart, n., a. suddhiprakarn, i. kheoruenromne and r.j. gilkes.  2010.  layer-charge characteristics of smectite in thai vertisols.  clays clay miner. 58 (2): 247-262.
chittamart, n., a. suddhiprakarn, i. kheoruenromne and r.j. gilkes.  2010.  properties of smectite in thai vertisols.  in rgj-phd congress xi, 1-3rd april.  jomtien palm beach resort, pattaya, chonburi, thailand.
chittamart, n., a. suddhiprakarn, i. kheoruenromne and r.j. gilkes.  2009.  fertility status of lowland vertisols in central plain.  in the proceeding of 35th congress on science and technology of thailand, 15-17th october.  chonburi, thailand.
