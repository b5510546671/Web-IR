grading

grades are assigned as the following: 
 
f d d+ c c+ b b+ a
<50 50-56 57-63 64-71 72-78 79-84 85-89 >90
 
scores at the boundary could be pushed up from student's performance during class period, contributions towards classroom discussions and attendance. 

scores are weighted as the following: 

  • 30%: exam : final
  • 30%: exam : midterm
  • 15%: quiz i
  • 15%: quiz ii
  • 10%: assignments

examination and quizzes: there are 4 major tests, two in-class quizzes, one midterm and one final examination. quick and short quizzes will be arranged in class and announced later.

homework: hard copy of homeworks are to be submitted on time and will not accepted later without compelling case.

assignments: to be announced.
