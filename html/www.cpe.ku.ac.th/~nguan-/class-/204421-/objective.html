course objectives

this course is designed for undergraduate students in a computer engineering program who have a knowledge of basic data communications and local area network technology such as ethernet,  token ring, and fddi. course prerequisite is 204325 introduction computer communication and networks. we will concentrate our studies on the transmission control protocol/ internet protocol  (tcp/ip). it covers tcp/ip protocol suite and its applications, routing protocol and internetworking design concepts.
